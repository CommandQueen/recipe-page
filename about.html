First Interesting Dish, Samp and Beans (Umngqusho).
Ingredients: 1 cups samp and beans washed and soaked overnight 14 ml cooking oil 0 onion chopped 0 green pepper chopped 0 tbsp Rajah Mild & Spicy curry powder 0 tbsp Robertsons steak and chops spice 0 Knorrox beef stock cube
Cook: It is important to soak the beans in water overnight. This ensures that the beans cook more evenly and do not split or separate from the skin. When cooking, do not stir too much, the beans will become starchy. Also check to make sure that the beans do not stick to the bottom of the saucepan.

The first two images show Samp and Beans, one of the most healthy and popular Xhosa dishes. The dish is full of fibre, which assist in improving gut health.
