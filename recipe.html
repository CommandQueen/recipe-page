Chicken-Feet (Amanqina)

Ingredients 1 onion 1 green pepper Chicken stock cubes x2 1 teaspoon chicken spice 1 teaspoon mild and spicy curry powder 1 kg of chicken feet
Method: Wash and season the chicken feet. Chop your desired vegetables and set aside. Lightly coat the bottom of a large and thick skillet with vegetable oil bring to a high heat Add the seasoned chicken feet to the hot oil and saute on high for about 5 minutes. Stirring in between, Add about a cup of water for every pound of chicken feet, along with the cut vegetables and simmer on low heat for about 2 hours. You may need to add water in between to ensure there is liquid for cooking and prevent burning.


Hard-Body Chicken (Umleqwa)

Ingriedients
Full chicken (hard body) 2 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper 2 chicken stock cube Chopped onion Water
Method
Clean the chicken, put it in a pot. Cook without adding water. The chicken will start producing its own oil. Brown the chicken and add the chopped onion.. Add salt,cayenne pepper, chicken stock. Mix well, Now add boilling water, make sure the water covers the the meat. (i used the 1,7 liter) Cook the chicken until the liquid is half, add more water to cover the chicken. Now cook it until the liquid has chicken..and just enough gravy.
